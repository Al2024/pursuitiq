REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL

Project: Downtown Office Complex Renovation
Client: TechCorp Industries
Location: 123 Business District, Metro City

PROJECT OVERVIEW:
TechCorp Industries is seeking proposals for the complete renovation of our downtown office complex. The project involves modernizing 50,000 square feet of office space across 5 floors.

REQUIRED DISCIPLINES:
- Architecture (space planning, interior design)
- Structural Engineering (foundation assessment, seismic upgrades)
- MEP Engineering (HVAC, electrical, plumbing systems)
- Civil Engineering (site improvements, parking)
- Landscape Architecture (outdoor spaces)

KEY DATES:
- RFP Release: September 15, 2025
- Pre-bid Meeting: October 1, 2025
- Site Visit: October 15, 2025
- Proposal Due: November 15, 2025
- Contract Award: December 1, 2025
- Project Completion: June 30, 2026

PROJECT REQUIREMENTS:
1. LEED Gold certification required
2. Seismic retrofit to current building codes
3. Modern open office concept
4. Energy-efficient systems
5. Accessible design compliance

POTENTIAL RISKS:
- Historic building constraints
- Working in occupied building
- Tight construction schedule
- Complex MEP coordination
- Regulatory approval delays
- Budget constraints

BUDGET: $15-20 million
DURATION: 9 months

Please submit your qualifications and approach by November 15, 2025.
